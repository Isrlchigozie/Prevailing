About Our Institute

Established in 2004, we've been providing quality computer education to the Umuahia community and beyond.

Our History

Prevailing Computers started in June 2004 at No. 3 Club Road Umuahia with just 7 pioneer students. Due to high demand and rapid growth, we relocated to our current location at No. 30 Ohafia Street, Umuahia, where we continue to provide excellent computer training.
